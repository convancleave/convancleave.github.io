Not another drum lesson website...
The world doesn't need another drum lesson website. The amount of exercises, licks, and drum lessons out there is overwhelming. We have all of these tools and little to no guidance on how to use them. The truth is, what you practice is less important than how you practice.
The Van Cleave Method is an approach to practicing that draws on the wisdom of many different domains, including the study of other instruments, strength training, sports, martial arts, and many other artistic and techincal pursuits.
What we teach
  • How to practice in a way that improves your overall playing, rather than praciticing to master exercises.
  • How to develop your own vocabulary and free your playing of preorchestrated licks.
  • How to develop an excess of technique, giving you the freedom to play with speed, precision, and dynamic control on any surface.
  • How to come up with your own creative grooves to fit any musical situation.
  • How to develop your Creativity, Originality, and Improvisational skills.
  • How to internalize subdivisions and develop a great sense of time.
  • How to develop touch, dynamics, and a great sound.
  • How to develop the freedom to play new ideas on the spot and remove the "latency" between idea and execution.
  • How to maintain the skills you've already aquired while developing new skills.
  • How to tactically target and eliminate weak links.
